New hires receive onboarding instructions through their registered personal email before joining. The onboarding checklist includes identity verification, laptop collection or shipment, corporate email activation, HR profile completion, security awareness training, and benefits enrollment.

On the first day, employees should confirm access to email, Teams, the HR portal, timesheet system, VPN, and required project tools. Any missing access should be reported through the onboarding support ticket category with the hiring manager copied.
