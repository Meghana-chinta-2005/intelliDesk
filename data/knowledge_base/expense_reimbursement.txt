Expense reimbursement claims must be submitted in the finance portal within thirty days of the expense date. Attach clear receipts, select the correct cost center, and include the business purpose for travel, meals, software, or client-related spending. Claims without receipts may be returned for correction.

Manager approval is required before Finance processes payment. Domestic reimbursements are usually paid in the next payroll cycle after approval, while international travel claims may take additional review time for currency conversion and policy validation.
