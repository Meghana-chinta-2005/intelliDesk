Payroll questions should be raised through the HR payroll helpdesk with the payslip month, employee ID, and a short description of the issue. Common payroll queries include missing allowances, tax declaration updates, bank account changes, salary hold questions, and reimbursement payment status.

Payroll corrections are handled according to the payroll calendar. If a correction misses the current cutoff, it may appear in the next cycle. Employees should not share full bank account numbers or tax IDs in email; use the secure HR portal attachment option.
