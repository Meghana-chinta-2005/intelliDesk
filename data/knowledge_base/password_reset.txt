Employees can reset their network password through the self-service password portal using registered MFA. The new password must be at least fourteen characters and should not reuse recent passwords, employee name, date of birth, project name, or simple keyboard patterns.

After changing the password, update saved credentials on VPN, Outlook, Teams, mobile email, and browser profiles. If the reset portal is unavailable or MFA cannot be completed, contact the Service Desk for identity verification and a temporary reset.
