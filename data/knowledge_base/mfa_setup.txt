Multi-factor authentication is required for email, VPN, HR systems, finance tools, and cloud applications. Employees should register the Microsoft Authenticator app as the primary method and add a backup phone number to avoid being locked out during device changes.

When replacing a phone, update MFA methods before wiping the old device. If the old device is already unavailable, contact the Service Desk for identity verification and MFA reset. Never approve an MFA prompt that you did not initiate.
