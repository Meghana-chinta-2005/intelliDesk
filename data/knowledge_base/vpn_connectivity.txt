VPN access is required when employees connect to internal systems from outside the office network. Before raising a ticket, confirm that the internet connection is stable, the VPN client is updated, MFA is working, and the account password has not expired.

If VPN connects but internal apps do not load, capture the VPN gateway name, error message, device hostname, and affected application. Frequent disconnects may be caused by weak Wi-Fi, blocked ports, expired certificates, or endpoint compliance checks.
