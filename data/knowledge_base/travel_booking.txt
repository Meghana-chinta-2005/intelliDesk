Business travel must be approved before tickets or hotels are booked. Employees should submit the travel request with destination, dates, client or project purpose, estimated cost, and manager approval. International travel may require additional immigration, insurance, and security review.

Use the approved travel desk for flights, hotels, and ground transport. Personal upgrades, leisure extensions, and changes made outside the travel desk may not be reimbursable unless they are pre-approved and documented in the travel request.
