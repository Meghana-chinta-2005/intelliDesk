Standard accessories such as keyboard, mouse, headset, laptop charger, HDMI adapter, and laptop bag can be requested through the IT asset portal. Employees must provide a business reason, delivery location, and manager approval when the item is not part of the standard onboarding kit.

Lost or damaged accessories should be reported immediately. IT may issue a replacement after validating asset history and availability. Repeated loss or non-standard accessory requests may require department head approval.
