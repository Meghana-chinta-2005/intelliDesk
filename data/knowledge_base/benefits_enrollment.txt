New hires can enroll in benefits during the first thirty calendar days after their joining date. Annual benefits changes are handled during the open enrollment window announced by HR each year. Employees should review medical, dental, vision, life insurance, and nominee details before submitting their choices.

After the enrollment window closes, changes are allowed only for qualifying life events such as marriage, birth or adoption of a child, loss of other coverage, or legal separation. Submit the HR benefits request with supporting documentation within thirty days of the event.
